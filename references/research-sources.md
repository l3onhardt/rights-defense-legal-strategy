# Research Sources

Use current, official, or authoritative sources before giving legal conclusions in mainland China matters. Do not rely on memory where current law, local practice, or platform rules can change the result.

## Primary Official Sources

- National Laws and Regulations Database: `https://flk.npc.gov.cn/`
- The State Council / China Government Network policy and regulation search: `https://www.gov.cn/`
- Supreme People's Court: `https://www.court.gov.cn/`
- Supreme People's Procuratorate: `https://www.spp.gov.cn/`
- Ministry of Justice: `https://www.moj.gov.cn/`
- China Legal Service Network / 12348: `https://www.12348.gov.cn/`
- National public legal service and local judicial bureau sites for legal aid, mediation, notarization, arbitration, and administrative reconsideration guidance.

## Courts, Cases, and Enforcement

- People's Court online service portals and local court filing guidance.
- China Judgments Online: `https://wenshu.court.gov.cn/`
- China Enforcement Information Online: `http://zxgk.court.gov.cn/`
- People's Court Case Database when available through official court channels.

Use cases as persuasive or practical guidance, not as a substitute for statutes, judicial interpretations, or binding procedural rules. Confirm whether a cited case is current, similar, and from a relevant court level.

## Market, Consumer, Company, and Platform Disputes

- National 12315 Platform: `https://www.12315.cn/`
- State Administration for Market Regulation and local market supervision bureaus.
- National Enterprise Credit Information Publicity System: `https://www.gsxt.gov.cn/`
- Local consumer associations and market supervision complaint channels.
- Platform terms, community rules, merchant rules, appeal rules, privacy rules, and fee rules from the platform's official site or in-app rule center.

## Labor, Housing, Finance, Data, and Other Regulators

Prefer the relevant ministry, commission, regulator, or local authority:

- Human resources and social security departments for labor contracts, wages, social insurance, work injury, labor arbitration, and employment disputes.
- Housing and urban-rural development departments for rent, property management, construction, and real estate issues.
- Cyberspace administrations and data/privacy regulators for personal information, account, algorithm, content, and data processing issues.
- Financial regulators, central bank branches, and industry associations for banking, payment, insurance, securities, private lending, or collection issues.
- Public security official channels for fraud, threats, illegal detention, identity misuse, cybercrime, or public order matters.
- Tax, customs, education, health, transport, culture/tourism, and other local departments when the issue falls under their authority.

## Verification Checklist

For each authority used, record:

```text

```

## Reliability Order

1. Current statutes, regulations, judicial interpretations, and official procedural rules.
2. Official regulator/court/government guidance and filing portals.
3. Published cases, guiding cases, court examples, and authoritative commentaries.
4. Platform rules and contract text for platform/contract issues.
5. Law firm articles, media reports, forum posts, and AI summaries only as clues for further verification.

Never use unofficial summaries as final authority without checking the underlying source.

## Search Patterns

Use targeted searches:

- `<issue> site:flk.npc.gov.cn`
- `<law name> 修订 生效 site:flk.npc.gov.cn`
- `<procedure> <city/province> 法院 立案`
- `<issue> 司法解释 最高人民法院`
- `<platform name> 规则 申诉 条款`
- `<regulator> 投诉 指南 <province/city>`

When the issue is local, include province/city and check local court, bureau, arbitration commission, or regulator pages.
